NIST_VECTORSArray

Test vectors provided by NIST. - P: the prime modulus. - Q: the prime order of a subgroup of GF(P). - G: the generator of the subgroup of order Q. - M: the message to be signed. - X: the private key. - Y: the public key. - K: the per-message nonce. - (R, S): the signature.
